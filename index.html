Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our property management services

We proudly serve the entire Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, including Dallas, Plano, Arlington, Fort Worth, and surrounding areas. Our local expertise allows us to effectively manage properties throughout the region.

We offer competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Our management fee is a straightforward percentage of collected rent. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your property.

Our rigorous screening process includes credit checks, background checks, employment verification, income verification (minimum 3x rent), and rental history verification. We only place qualified tenants who meet our strict criteria.

We have an in-house maintenance team and a network of licensed contractors, including licensed HVAC technicians and plumbers. We offer 24/7 emergency maintenance support and handle all repairs promptly to protect your investment.

You'll receive detailed monthly financial statements, and you have 24/7 access to your owner portal where you can view real-time information about your property, including rent payments, maintenance requests, and documents.

We have a strict rent collection policy with late fees and follow-up procedures. If necessary, we handle the entire eviction process professionally and legally, working with experienced attorneys to protect your interests.
